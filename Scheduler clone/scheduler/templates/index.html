How categories work
Categories let you tag events beyond the default Personal / Work / Other labels.
When creating or launching an event, choose your custom category from the dropdown.
The category colour shows as a dot on every event row and in the day report.
Built-in categories (Personal, Work, Other) cannot be deleted.
